### Hotkeys

- <https://youtu.be/cDcoBMVJsvk>

- Alternar Edit-Preview mode - `ctrl-e`
- Open Quick Switcher - `ctrl-o` (useful to open files)
- Search - `ctrl-shitf-f`
- Back - `alt-left`
- Forward - `alt-right`
- New note - `ctrl-n`
